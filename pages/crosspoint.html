Crosspoint

Following on from Optiverse, Erik Verlage moved straight on to developing Crosspoint as his second educational game with the Gamkedo club. This time the intention was to teach basic vector mathematics, up to and including the dot and cross products. I once again contributed a large number of levels to the project, some of which even stumped Erik himself, for a while!

  • Play at: Gamkedo
  • Developed: June-August 2016
  • Main role: Level design
