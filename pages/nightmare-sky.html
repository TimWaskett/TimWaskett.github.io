Nightmare Sky

A major part of Critical Alpha was the procedural terrain system. I built it with the assistance of Sebastian Lague's YouTube tutorial series but modified it to improve its performance and make it a bit more stable when travelling large distances. Chris DeLeon took this modified terrain system, and the procedural cloud system I'd also developed for Critical Alpha, to form the basis of Nightmare Sky. The priciple game mechanic was jumping from cloud to cloud over an infinite terrain, trying to escape from nightmare monsters while attempting to collect your lost toys.

  • Play at: Gamkedo
  • Developed: August-September 2016
  • Main role: Terrain and cloud system programming
