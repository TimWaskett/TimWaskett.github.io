Singed Dirt

After the success of Divine Disaster, Jeremy Kenyon led another Unity project that made use of the terrain deformation system I had built for it. Singed Dirt is a 3D reimagining of the old 2D game Scorched Earth, where opposing players attempt to hit each other's tanks over a mountainous terrain. Because the terrain system I'd developed for Divine Disaster was so self contained it could be easily dropped into Singed Dirt to perform a similar function, namely creating craters where the projectiles impact the terrain. The system was also used to create the mountains at the start of each round, so that they grow out of the initially flat plane, one after the other.

  • Developed: January-March 2017
