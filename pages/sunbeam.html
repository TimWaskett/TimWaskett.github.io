Sunbeam

In brief, Sunbeam is a first-person puzzle/exploration game, inspired by games like The Witness and The Talos Principle. It is set on a small planet with a series of puzzles involving using mirrors to direct sunlight onto solar panels. As you progress through the game, you will unravel the mysteries behind the world and how you came to be there. What are all these mirrors and solar panels intended for? Who has been leaving message pods lying around, and what happened to them? You are free to explore at your own pace and as you solve the gently taxing logic puzzles more and more of the world opens up for you.
