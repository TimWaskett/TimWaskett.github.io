Sunbeam

My current project. Sunbeam is still in development but in brief it is a first-person puzzle/exploration game, inspired by games like The Witness and The Talos Principle. It is set on a small planet with a series of puzzles involving using mirrors to direct sunlight onto solar panels. The screenshots and gifs on this page are work-in-progress, to give an idea of the style and setting.
