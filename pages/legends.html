Legends of the Outlands

Legends of the Outlands was an ambitious project led by Leiah Fournier in the Gamkedo club. It is a networked co-op puzzle adventure game with a pair of rats as the protagonists, following them on their journey into the outside world after all humans disappear. I provided various kitchen object models including the toaster, weighing scales, pin cushion, milk jug, paper towel holder and window latch. This was the first project I contributed a significant amount of 3D art to.

  • Play at: Gamkedo
  • Developed: January-February 2016
  • Main role: 3D modelling
