About

I've been making games in my free time since 2010. Nowadays I use the Unity game engine but started by coding native Android games. I consider myself a designer-programmer and have made a number of games both on my own and as a collaboration. In particular, I have led several projects in the Gamkedo club and contributed to many more.

Although I specialise in coding, I also created many of the 3D art assets you'll see in my portfolio, using Blender. The low-poly flat-shaded style allows me to create recognisable assets in a short time. While not as proficient at art as I am at coding, I enjoy it nonetheless. Say hello on Twitter, or visit my itch.io page using the links below.

Critical Alpha

Developed as a collaborative project in the Gamkedo club. An arcade combt flight sim with procedurally generated landscape and missions across three time periods. I was the project lead, doing the majority of the coding and a large part of the 3D model creation. I wanted to recreate the feeling of the simple combat flight sims I loved to play in the early 90s, in particular the up-close dog-fighting that was so much fun.
