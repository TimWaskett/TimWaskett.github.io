Clever Girl

My first big 3D Unity game. Clever Girl was the also the first time I'd managed a group of eight other remote contributers. It was a very ambitious project but it came together far better than I could have hoped. This was my attempt at a cinematic twin-stick shooter style game, with the main protagonist using a bow and arrow to fight off dinosaurs, while trying to repair her broken time machine. It also features the voice work of my wife, bringing Captain Kat to life.
